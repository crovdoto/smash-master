\paragraph{King K. Rool}

\subparagraph{Combos}

\[
	uair \to dair \to \begin{cases}
		grab	\\	dsmash	\\ \hdots
	\end{cases}
\]
This can function as a touch-of-death string:\\

\[
	uair \to dair \to grab dthrow \to nair \to DA \to bridge \to finisher
